About Cebu Sacred Heart College -Carcar campus

Nurturing minds and hearts since 2005

Our Mission

To enhance virtue, developed competence, promote excellence, and inspire service in all academic levels of the institution.

Our Vision

The Sacred Heart College envisions to produce graduates who are Christ-centered, Critical thinkers, Service oriented and Globally Competitive.

Goals

  1. Consistent pursuit of academic excellence.
  2. Faithful adherence to christian values and virtue.
  3. Learning environment conductive to holistic formation of human capabilities.
  4. Continuous faculty development to promote academic excellence.
  5. Promotion of academic and cultural development.
  6. Partnership with the community in providing sustainable, basic social service, and program towards self-sufficiency and self-reliance.
  7. Strict compliance of mandates of the Department of Education and other relevant government agencies.
  8. In conformity with the K to 12 Standards & competencies.

CORE VALUES

  • Integrity
  • Christ-centered
  • Excellence

How to Enroll

1

Visit campus

Come to any of our campuses and proceed to the Registrar's Office

2

Submit Requirements

Provide all necessary documents and fill out enrollment forms

3

Assessment

4
